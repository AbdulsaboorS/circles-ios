# Phase 06.1 Plan 02: AppBackground Summary

**One-liner:** Animated dual-blob breathing background using Ellipse+blur with @Environment colorScheme — dark (#0E0B08/#1A3A2A) and light (#F5F0E8/#EDE0C8) modes, no ThemeManager dependency.

## What Was Built

`AppBackground` — a standalone SwiftUI view that renders two organic blob shapes on a solid background, breathing slowly through opacity and scale animations. The two blobs use asymmetric durations (4s primary, 5s secondary) and a 1.2s delay offset so they never animate in sync, producing the meditative organic feel specified in design decision D-09.
