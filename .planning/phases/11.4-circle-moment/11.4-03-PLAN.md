- Change the card background so corners only show on the NON-photo areas OR keep RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 0) on photo, cornerRadius 16 on overall card and clip

The simplest correct approach:
- Keep the outer VStack's `.background(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 16).fill(...))` and `.clipShape(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 16))`
- The photo block: change the AsyncImage frame from `frame(height: 420)` to `frame(maxWidth: .infinity).aspectRatio(3.0/4.0, contentMode: .fill)`
- Remove any `.padding(.horizontal, ...)` from the photo ZStack itself
- The photo will be clipped by the outer `.clipShape(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 16))` which gives it rounded top corners naturally
