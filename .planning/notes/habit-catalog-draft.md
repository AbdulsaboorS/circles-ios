## Per-spirituality rationale variants (8 entries)

For these 8 entries, the rationale shifts based on the user's spirituality answer:
- `gentle` → shown to **J** (and **B** where the entry isn't tagged for J)
- `default` (above) → shown to **B** and **S**
- `ambitious` → shown to **D**

Where a variant isn't listed, the default rationale is used for all tagged levels. The vast majority of entries (36/44) use the default for everyone — variants only exist where the framing genuinely changes.
